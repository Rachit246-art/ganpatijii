What We Offer

Div Mangal Murtis provides four core services, each tailored to bring elegance and sustainability to your devotional space.

Eco‑Friendly Clay Murti

Hand‑crafted from locally sourced, biodegradable clay, our idols blend tradition with green living.

Custom Sculpting

Personalised designs to match your vision – from bespoke poses to unique facial expressions.

Restoration & Repainting

Revive treasured family idols with meticulous restoration and colour‑matching repainting.

Safe Transit & Home Setup

Secure packaging and expert placement guidance to protect your idol during moving.
