Our Heritage

A Legacy Carved
in Devotion

Div Mangal Murtis was founded with a single, unwavering belief — that every Ganesha idol should carry within it the soul of its maker. Over the last 25 years, our master artisans have hand-carved thousands of idols, each one a unique expression of devotion, beauty, and skilled craftsmanship.

We began in a small workshop in Pune, where our founder Shri Mangal Das first learned the ancient art of murti-making from his grandfather. That workshop grew — not through compromise, but through a relentless commitment to quality and an ever-deepening love for the craft.

Today, Div Mangal Murtis serves devotees across India and internationally, yet every idol still passes through the same careful hands and the same heartfelt quality check that has defined us from day one.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Core Values

We exist to bridge the ancient art of murti-making with modern sensibilities — creating sacred, sustainable pieces that live beautifully in today's homes.

Eco-Conscious

Every idol is crafted from sustainably sourced clay and natural pigments. Our eco-friendly murtis dissolve gracefully during Visarjan, protecting our rivers and ecosystems.

Artisan Devotion

Our artisans are not just craftspeople — they are devotees. Each idol is shaped with prayer and intention, infusing every piece with spiritual energy and reverence.

Premium Quality

From the finest clay to the richest gold leaf, we never compromise on materials. Every Div Mangal idol is a timeless heirloom crafted to be passed down through generations.

Custom Craftsmanship

Your Ganesha should be uniquely yours. We offer fully bespoke sculpting — choose your pose, size, finish, and decorative details to create your perfect divine centerpiece.

Safe Delivery

Our white-glove transit and home-setup service ensures your idol arrives in perfect condition. We custom-pack and personally supervise installations for select orders.

Restoration & Care

We offer expert restoration and repainting for heirloom murtis, breathing new life into idols that carry decades of family memory and devotion.
